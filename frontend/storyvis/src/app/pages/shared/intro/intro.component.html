INTRODUCTION TO THE STUDY


The following is an outline of this user study.


First, you will have unlimited practice time to become familiar with the tool before starting the actual evaluation (the recommended practice time is about 15 minutes).

During the actual evaluation, you have unlimited time to complete the following tasks:

1) Explore the imaging data to find all nodules/anomalies in it.
2) Measure the diameter of all the nodules/anomalies found in the imaging data.
3) Create annotations and/or make additional measurements on the nodules/anomalies found in the imaging data.
4) Create a report to communicate your findings to collaborators.

Finally, we ask you to complete a short questionnaire at the end.



(Note that we are not testing you, we are testing our techniques)
